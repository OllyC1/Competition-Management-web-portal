Welcome to the UK E-Sports League

Join us for an exciting national e-sports competition where individuals and teams compete in various gaming categories to be crowned the ultimate e-sports champions.

The event features ten mini pop-up gaming tournaments across major UK cities, each lasting 3 days with high-tech gaming setups tracking players' scores, in-game performance metrics, and overall rankings.

Tournament Format

Ten mini pop-up gaming tournaments across major UK cities
Each tournament lasts for 3 days
High-tech gaming setups tracking performance metrics
Participants compete as part of a team in one tournament
Prizes awarded in categories such as age, gender, and team performance
Winners advance to the national finals
